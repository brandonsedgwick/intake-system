Idea 1: Slide-out Panel

A slide-out panel that appears from the right side when you click "Open Communications" on any client. The main outreach dashboard remains visible but dimmed. Panel can be resized or closed. Similar to: Gmail compose, Slack threads, Intercom

Idea 2: Full-Screen Modal

A full-screen modal that takes over the entire view when opened. Provides maximum space for reading and composing emails. Has a dedicated layout optimized for email communication. Similar to: Outlook compose, HubSpot email, Zendesk tickets

Idea 3: Expandable Split View

Keep the current tab-based approach but make the communications section expandable. Click "Expand" to take over the full right pane. Collapsible sections for thread vs compose. Similar to: Notion, Linear, modern SaaS tools

Summary & Recommendation

Idea 1: Slide-out

Best for quick interactions while keeping dashboard visible

Idea 2: Full Modal ⭐

Best for focused email work with maximum space

Idea 3: Split View

Best balance of context and space

My recommendation: Idea 2 (Full Modal) provides the best experience for composing and reading emails. The two-column layout with thread on left and compose on right is intuitive and gives proper space to both activities. However, if staying in context is important, Idea 3 offers a nice compromise.
